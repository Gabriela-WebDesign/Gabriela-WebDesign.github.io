Little Swimmer 🏊

Little Gabby since she was born was curious of water 💦
As little as she was, she got into the pool
Thanks to her grandpa, who built the pools for a living
She had one just outside in her yard
She came back home to Poland and lived her best life
Swimming was in her veins since her mom was a swimmer 🏊
She went to school and in second grade she had swim lessons
Since she was swimming from basically being born she was getting bored
Her energy was to much to handle at home, so her mom looked for a team to join
She found one called "Gim 92" and made her join
She started training for real and competig at meets
She grew up fast and made it to nationals
She won one medal, then a second, a third
Medal after a medal 🏅
Gold after gold
The story went on
She got her chance to represent the country
Met so many nice people along the way
Till this day she's embracing all the moments
And doesn't stop following her dream
Now she's still swimming, competing and thrilling
She finds her peace in the silence of water 💞


Gabriela Herbreder
